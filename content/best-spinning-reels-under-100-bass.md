## What Matters Most in a Spinning Reel

When buying a reel under $100, focus on:

- **Drag quality** — Smoothness matters more than maximum drag rating. A jerky drag costs you fish.
- **Handle comfort** — You'll be cranking this all day.
- **Overall weight** — Lighter is better for all-day fishing and sensitivity.
- **Brand reliability** — Pflueger, Shimano, Penn, and Lew's all stand behind their products.
