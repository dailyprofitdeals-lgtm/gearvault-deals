## What to Look For in a Budget Combo

When shopping for combos under $150, focus on these factors:

- **Smooth drag** — This matters more than anything else. A jerky drag loses fish.
- **Rod sensitivity** — Avoid the absolute cheapest blanks. You need to feel the bite.
- **Handle comfort** — You'll be holding this all day.
- **Reliable brand** — Pflueger, Shimano, Lew's, Penn, and Ugly Stik all stand behind their products.
